DeGoyler MacNaughton Conduct Prospect Evaluation

  • August 5, 2014
  • By Amanda H.

Lockhart has hired DeGolyer and MacNaughton, a leading independent consulting firm focused on the petroleum industry to complete a prospect evaluation on the Raton Basin Play. This comprehensive report details and highlights the formations in the field and the possible reserves available as far as recoverable and non-recoverable oil and gas deposits. Their conservative estimation practices ensure a thorough, unbiased report which gives investors an accurate pulse on the practical value of an asset. In regards to the Raton Basin, 10 key formations have been researched and documented, showing the potential for each formation to produce key resources.
